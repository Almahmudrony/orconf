An open source digital design conference

We're pleased to announce that ORCONF 2015 will be held over the weekend of October 10 and 11 at Ideasquare, CERN in Geneva, Switzerland.

ORCONF is an open source digital design and embedded systems conference, covering areas of electronics from the transistor level up to Linux user space and beyond. Primarily we'll hear about open source IP projects and implementations on FPGA and silicon, EDA tools, licensing and embedded software to name a few. Begun as the annual OpenRISC developers and users conference, it has become a general open source digital design conference.

The event will run from 12:00 until 19:30 on Saturday, and from 09:30 until 15:30 on Sunday. A dinner as a group will be arranged for the Saturday evening, and for those arriving on Friday evening there's usually a venue nominated for pre-event drinks. Lunch will be provided both days.
